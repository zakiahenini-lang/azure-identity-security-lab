## Policy Name

Require MFA for Cloud Administrators

---

## Purpose

This policy is designed to protect privileged access in Microsoft Entra ID.

Cloud administrator accounts are high-value targets because they can manage users, groups, access controls, security settings, and cloud resources.

The goal of this policy is to require Multi-Factor Authentication before privileged users can access cloud management portals.
